Vision

As the ANKA IT Committee, we aim to build a strong technology driven community for international students and recent graduates in the Netherlands. Our goal is to make IT innovation a way of life by providing a platform where members can design websites, develop backends, build apps, and contribute to secure, scalable solutions. Through hands on projects and collaborative initiatives, we help participants strengthen their technical skills, gain real-world experience, and grow into confident IT professionals ready to shape the digital future.
