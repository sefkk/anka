Marketing Committee

Mission and Objectives

The Marketing Committee acts as a bridge between ANKA community members and the outside world. It manages social media accounts and creates up-to-date content. Its goal is to represent ANKA accurately and effectively, connecting people with ANKA’s ideas, projects, and announcements.
