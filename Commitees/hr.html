HR Committee

Driving innovation and digital transformation through technology and technical support at ANKA.

Mission and Objectives

As the HR Committee, we work to manage the recruitment process of new members, strengthen relationships among existing members, and foster a dynamic and inclusive community culture. By utilizing our Talent Pool system efficiently, we align our members’ skills with the right areas and actively contribute to their personal and professional development.

Goals and Plans

Looking ahead, our aim is not only to enhance our internal structure but also to build collaborations with corporate HR departments to connect our members with valuable professional opportunities. By expanding our Talent Pool system to include companies, we plan to take strategic steps toward offering meaningful career connections and adding value to our members’ career journeys.
