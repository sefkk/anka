Law Committee

Driving innovation and digital transformation through technology and technical support at ANKA.

Goals and Plans

The committee focuses on providing legal guidance and support to community members. It aims to facilitate easy access to legal information, assist with career planning, and foster a legally aware community. Through workshops, seminars, and collaborative projects, we ensure members become informed about their rights and legal procedures.

Mission and Objectives

The committee’s mission is to help individuals overcome legal challenges and make legal knowledge accessible. By raising awareness and supporting informed decision-making processes, we build a solid foundation for the personal and professional growth of our members. The committee conducts solution-oriented legal work across a broad spectrum, from individual rights to commercial regulations.
